## 输出到文件
1. select ... into outfile ...

    select * from control_pagos
    into outfile 'c:\\data.csv' 
    FIELDS TERMINATED BY ',' 
    OPTIONALLY ENCLOSED BY '"'
    LINES TERMINATED BY '\n';
